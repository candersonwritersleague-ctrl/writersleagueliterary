Our Mission & Partnerships

**Writers League** is a group of Literary agents worldwide. We prioritize authors who have high potential to be acquired by major publishers. Our job is to guide, strategize and represent their book to our partnered Traditional Publishing houses, including Penguin Random House, HarperCollins, Macmillan, Scholastic, and Hachette Book Group. We also actively pursue adoption in movie producers/film makers like Netflix, The Walt Disney Studios, Warner Bros. Entertainment, and Universal Pictures. Furthermore, we are deeply committed to helping authors who have experienced abuse or neglect by their previous publishers, ensuring their work and careers are managed with integrity and respect.
